Baahubali is an Indian epic action film directed by S. S. Rajamouli, released in two parts: Baahubali: The Beginning (2015) and Baahubali: The Conclusion (2017). The movie stars Prabhas, Rana Daggubati, Anushka Shetty, Tamannaah, Ramya Krishnan, and Sathyaraj. It tells the story of the mighty kingdom of Mahishmati and the intense rivalry between two brothers, Amarendra Baahubali and Bhallaladeva. The first part focuses on Shiva, also known as Mahendra Baahubali, who gradually discovers his royal heritage, while the second part reveals how his father Amarendra Baahubali was betrayed and killed. Themes of bravery, betrayal, sacrifice, and justice run strongly throughout the story. Sivagami, the powerful queen mother, plays a central role in shaping the fate of the kingdom. The film is known for its grand battle scenes, rich visuals, and breathtaking special effects. The suspense of “Why Kattappa killed Baahubali?” became a nationwide sensation before the release of the second film. The powerful music composed by M. M. Keeravani added depth and grandeur to the storytelling. Originally made in Telugu and Tamil, the movie was later dubbed into several languages, reaching audiences across India and beyond. Baahubali broke many records to become one of the highest-grossing Indian films ever. It also brought international recognition to Indian cinema. Overall, the film is remembered as a landmark achievement in filmmaking for its scale, storytelling, and visual brilliance.

About Director

S. S. Rajamouli is a visionary Indian film director and screenwriter, celebrated for creating some of the biggest blockbusters in Indian cinema. He rose to fame with films like Magadheera, Eega, and achieved worldwide recognition through Baahubali: The Beginning, Baahubali: The Conclusion, and RRR. Known for his grand imagination, Rajamouli blends strong storytelling with spectacular visuals and powerful emotions, setting new benchmarks in filmmaking. His movies often portray themes of heroism, sacrifice, and cultural pride, presented on a massive scale. With his unique style of blending tradition with modern filmmaking techniques, he has elevated Indian cinema to the global stage. Today, Rajamouli is regarded as one of the most successful, influential, and respected directors in the film industry.

About Music Director



M. M. Keeravani is a legendary Indian music composer and playback singer, best known for his melodious tunes and powerful background scores. He has worked in Telugu, Tamil, Hindi, and several other languages, creating timeless music for films across industries. His collaborations with director S. S. Rajamouli, especially in movies like Magadheera, Eega, Baahubali, and RRR, brought him immense fame and global recognition. In 2023, he won the prestigious Golden Globe and Academy Award for the song Naatu Naatu from RRR, making history for Indian cinema. With his versatility and soulful compositions, Keeravani continues to be one of the most respected and influential music directors in India.
